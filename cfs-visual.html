HOW TO READ THIS

The inner ring shows what each month holds — the quality carried before motion begins. The outer ring shows what it expresses — what it produces when it moves.

When inner and outer are the same colour the month is a stabilizer — it locks that quality in place. When they differ, it is a converter — it turns one quality into another.

THE CREATE OVERLAY

Create is not a base quality and not a month. It is an overlay that fires at exactly three points where a held quality produces something genuinely new — something that could not exist without that specific base.

It always moves in a triangle between the three base qualities. Remove any one gate and the loop cannot close.

Organize creates Communication at May
Communication creates Leadership at September
Leadership creates Organization at January

Each node is both the source for the arrow leaving it and the result of the arrow arriving. The system is self-closing — what January creates feeds into the February opening of the next loop.
